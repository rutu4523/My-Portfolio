Information about flower

The small purple flowers bloom in early spring to bid goodbye to winter are crocuses. They thrive under full sun exposure and well-drained soils.

five flowers

  • LILAC: The Lilac is a hardy bush that blooms in the spring.
  • PETUNIAS: Petunias are colorful flowers that bloom from the spring through the fall.
  • GERANIUMS: There are several different types of Geraniums and most bloom from spring through the fall.
  • IRISES: Irises are unique and pretty flowers that are fairly easy to grow.
  • SLEEP WELL: The speedwell flower blooms in spring, summer, and early fall.
